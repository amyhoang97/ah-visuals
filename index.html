About

Hello there!

My name is Amy Hoang, and I am a graphic designer and photographer based in Los Angeles, California.

  • I love to connect with people, especially other creatives, and be inspired by their stories and perspectives. Thus, I have a strong affinity for travelling, especially in Asia!
  • I am inspired by so many artists and styles, including Hayao Miyazaki’s animations, Edgar Wright’s films, Wes Anderson’s art direction.
  • My goals are to tell stories through visuals that viewers can connect with and help foster a world of more open-mindedness, acceptance, and love for art.
